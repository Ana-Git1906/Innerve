About CA Program

Techfest, IIT Bombay is Asia's largest Science and Technology festival, and the CA Program is its backbone. We at Techfest aspire to reach out to college students across the globe to spark their interest in Science and Technology and provide them a platform to pursue the same. The CA Program helps us realize this vision. Here, the CAs build a network of students from 2500+ Indian and 500+ international colleges and learn about social media, marketing, event planning and execution. They are rewarded for fulfilling their responsibilities and motivated to strive to improve their personal skills continually. As Techfest celebrates its silver jubilee this year, the role of the CAs is more important than ever before and this is a great opportunity for us all to work together and make the 25th Techfest a grand success.

Guidelines

To register for the College Ambassador Program, simply sign in using your Google account

Find all your personal information, including your rank, points, and winnings

Check out the tasks, their points, deadlines, and status of completion

Get an Instagram shout-out and other rewards based on your monthly performance

Role of a College Ambassador

Represent Techfest at your college, and act as a link between the students of your college and Techfest

Ideate, plan, organize, and promote the events of Techfest through various mediums and channels

Strategize and experiment with innovative marketing and publicity strategies to drive up student participation

Engage in discussions with the other CAs and the Techfest Coordinators to bring new ideas to the fest

FAQs

As a College Ambassador, you will be the representative of Techfest at your institution. You will be the first point of contact for any student who requires information about Techfest and youll be responsible for assisting them as and when required.

To be eligible, all you need is a valid student ID and a whole lot of enthusiasm.

Click on the Google Sign In button to apply now! Once the registration is complete, you will not be able to change your email address. Allow pop-ups if prompted to do so.

As a College Ambassador, you will be required to share Techfests posts on all social media platforms and put up posters on your college notice boards. You will also be instrumental in conducting workshops and sessions under the aegis of Techfest, IIT Bombay on your campus.

You are expected to put in 1-2 hours every week on an average. Consistent commitment and sincerity towards the assigned tasks are imperative.

Yes, depending on the size of the college, Techfest can appoint multiple College Ambassadors.

You will upload proofs of your work on the CA portal, we will verify them and reward you with points, which are a measure of your progress. Please feel free to reach out to your mentors in case you have any queries.
